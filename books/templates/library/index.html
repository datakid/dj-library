{% blocktrans %}Windows on Australia: Perceptions In and Through Translation is a major interdisciplinary research project exploring the translation and reception of Australian literature in various languages. The project is being carried out by Translation Studies in the School of Languages, Cultures and Linguistics at Monash University in collaboration with the Postcolonial Studies Program at Free University Berlin.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "The project aims to advance understanding of Australia's global role in literary production and clarify the marketability of Australian cultural exports to non-English-speaking countries. By mapping these exchanges, research will inform publishers, literary agents, authors, professional translators, and members of the public about the development and evolution of trends, norms, and perceptions pertaining to Australia's cultural contributions." %}

{% trans "This website presents a bibliography of Australian literary texts and their translations into German, Spanish, Italian, Japanese and Chinese compiled by the researchers." %}
